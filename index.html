Our Story

14 Years of Crafting Extraordinary Spaces

Vensco Interior Inc was founded in 2010 by lead designer Marco Vensco with a singular mission: to bring honest, human-centered design to every project, regardless of scale or budget.


What began as a boutique studio has grown into a full-service design firm with 18 specialists across disciplines — yet our approach remains deeply personal. Every client receives direct attention from a senior designer, and every project reflects our founding conviction that the details are the design.


Over 640 completed projects across residential, commercial, and hospitality sectors have earned us recognition including the Regional Design Excellence Award (2019, 2022) and a place on the Annual 'Top 50 Interior Design Firms' list.

01
Human First

Every design decision begins with the person who will inhabit the space — their rhythms, needs, aspirations, and dreams.

02
Material Truth

We select materials that are authentic, durable, and beautiful — never superficial. Quality you can feel in every surface.

03
Timeless Vision

We design against trends. Our work is meant to be loved not just today, but ten, twenty, thirty years from now.

04
Transparent Process

No hidden costs, no surprises. Clear communication and full transparency at every stage of the design journey.
